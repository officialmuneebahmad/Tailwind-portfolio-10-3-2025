# About Muneeb Ahmad

Hi, I'm Muneeb Ahmad, a passionate Computer Science student with a deep interest in technology, artificial intelligence and programming. I specialize in system maintenance, web development, and data automation (using Excel), bringing both academic knowledge and hands-on technical expertise to every project.

My technical skills span from system troubleshooting and OS management to modern web development using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like Tailwind CSS. I'm proficient in programming languages including C, C++, and Python, with experience in Git/GitHub for collaborative development. I've created practical solutions like Excel-based inventory systems with VBA automation and database management using MS Access.

What drives me is technology's potential to solve real-world problems. I'm particularly fascinated by artificial intelligence and its transformative applications across industries. This passion, combined with strong problem-solving abilities and eagerness to learn, makes me adaptable in our rapidly evolving digital landscape.

I'm actively seeking opportunities to contribute to innovative projects where I can apply my technical skills while continuing to grow in computer science field and explore emerging technologies like AI.
